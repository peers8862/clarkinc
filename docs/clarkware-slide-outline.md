# Clarkware Slide Outline

## Slide 1: Clarkware In One Sentence

Clarkware is CLARK's facility-aware software family for industrial operations, built around workstation-level Integrated Production Environments that combine job context, machine-linked evidence, messaging, and auditable human-and-AI coordination.

## Slide 2: The Problem

- industrial work is still fragmented across tools, chats, notes, exports, and dashboards
- critical decisions often depend on context that is hard to recover later
- this weakens quality, trust, accountability, and speed

## Slide 3: The Product Insight

- Clarkware acts like a modern clerk or scribe for industrial work
- it records and relays the ongoing story of research, testing, assembly, quality, and coordination inside the facility
- the product centers on operational memory, not generic enterprise software

## Slide 4: The Wedge

- one workstation-level IPE
- one evidence import path
- one job-linked messaging model
- one auditable AI support loop
- one manager-facing reporting layer

## Slide 5: What An IPE Is

- industrial reinterpretation of the IDE
- unified environment for job context, notes, artifacts, messaging, machine-linked evidence, and AI assistance
- starts at the workstation, expands to the facility

## Slide 6: Core Version 1 Capabilities

- Theia-based workstation environment
- note and artifact capture
- test and diagnostics evidence import
- XMPP-based or aligned messaging and presence
- visible AI review states
- facility reporting from the same record

## Slide 7: Why This Is Believable

- Theia proves modular extensible workspaces
- XMPP proves identity-aware messaging and presence
- industrial platforms prove plant-floor connectivity and reporting demand
- connected-worker platforms prove frontline workflow demand
- industrial AI platforms prove appetite for bounded AI support

## Slide 8: Why CLARK Has The Right To Build It

- Clarkware is anchored in CLARK's node model, not a detached SaaS idea
- Niagara Assembly gives a concrete first deployment environment
- the software strengthens licensing, governance, and repeatability across nodes

## Slide 9: Niagara Pilot

- first station: diagnostics or test workstation
- first deliverables: IPE, evidence adapter, job-linked threads, AI summary, reporting view
- first proof: live jobs, live evidence, live auditability

## Slide 10: Technical Advantage If It Works

- stronger process memory
- auditable human and AI participation
- better reporting and decision speed
- higher switching costs tied to operational record value
- repeatable node software package for future expansion

## Slide 11: Roadmap

- workstation proof
- facility visibility
- station expansion
- node standardization
- cross-node intelligence
- deeper automation later

## Slide 12: The Ask Or Closing

Clarkware is not trying to replace the whole factory stack on day one. It is proving a narrow but compounding workstation wedge that can become the memory and coordination layer of the CLARK network.
