## Short Answer Guidance

6. **OEM vs ODM**

An OEM owns the product brand and market relationship. An ODM both designs and manufactures a product that another company may sell under its own brand.

7. **Two ecosystem nodes**

Accept any two of:

- bare board fabricator
- component manufacturer
- component distributor
- design house
- test house
- logistics provider

8. **Why SMT strengthened EMS**

Strong answer themes:

- SMT required expensive equipment and process expertise
- shared capital investment favored manufacturers serving many customers
- higher automation and throughput rewarded scale and utilization

9. **Post-shortage behavior**

Accept answers such as:

- carrying more buffer inventory
- using long-term agreements
- approving alternates
- designing for component availability
- reducing single-source dependency

10. **How CLARK is positioned**

CLARK is positioned as a node-network operator and training provider within the North American electronics manufacturing landscape, focused on distributed capability, quality consistency, and workforce development rather than pure mega-scale volume.
