## Section 4: Ecosystem Mapping Exercise

### Scenario

A company sells a wireless industrial sensor that monitors machine vibration in a factory. The product is used in a safety-sensitive industrial environment. The company owns the brand and product design, but it does not own a factory.

### Task

Map the likely ecosystem for this product. Identify:

- who owns the product design
- who fabricates the PCB
- who supplies the components
- who assembles the board
- who performs testing
- who buys the finished product
- what quality level or regulatory expectations might apply
