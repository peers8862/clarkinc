# Hamilton Co-Location Draft Terms

## Purpose

This document translates the term sheet outline into working draft positions that are specific enough for agreement drafting.

## Status

- Status: `Working draft positions`
- Intended use: `Founder alignment, negotiation preparation, legal drafting input`

## Parties

- CLARK: training operator, curriculum owner, Clarkware developer, and activation lead for the CLARK-led portions of the launch footprint
- Niagara Assembly: host facility operator, back-third testing-and-flashing hub operator, and co-location partner

## Term

- Initial term: `24 months`
- Renewal: one automatic `12-month` renewal unless either party gives at least `90 days` notice before term end
- Early termination: for uncured material breach, insolvency, loss of site control, or failure to maintain required insurance

## Space

- Covered footprint: first-phase `4,500 sq ft` area at `24 Clark Ave`
- CLARK-led area: front two portions used for training, Clark Courses, demonstration, storage, and mixed teaching-and-testing use aligned with CLARK's launch model
- Niagara Assembly reserved area: back third used for specialized low-batch testing and flashing
- Additional `5,000 sq ft` main-level area: excluded from current CLARK rights unless later documented separately

## Shared Areas And Operating Rules

- Shared areas should include entrance access, washrooms, break areas, receiving paths, and designated storage or network utility areas as later marked on a site plan.
- Shared-area use should follow Niagara Assembly site rules unless a CLARK-specific training protocol is stricter.
- Niagara Assembly owns facility safety procedures, emergency response rules, and site access control.
- CLARK owns classroom conduct, instructor supervision, and training-process controls inside CLARK-led operations.

## Economics

### Training Revenue Participation

- `1 to 2 courses/month`: `CAD 1,250` fixed monthly fee plus `12%` of contribution margin after direct instructional costs
- `3 to 4 courses/month`: `CAD 2,500` fixed monthly fee plus `10%` of contribution margin after direct instructional costs
- `5+ courses/month`: `CAD 4,000` fixed monthly fee plus `8%` of contribution margin after direct instructional costs

### Direct Instructional Costs

Included:

- instructor compensation
- instructor travel and lodging where applicable
- course-specific consumables and materials
- third-party certification or delivery fees tied directly to a class

Excluded:

- CLARK overhead
- curriculum development
- business development
- general company administration

### Non-Training Revenue Treatment

- On-site Clark Courses cohorts delivered inside the CLARK-led footprint should be treated the same as training revenue for facility-fee purposes.
- Off-site partner-venue training should be excluded from Niagara Assembly revenue share unless Niagara Assembly resources are materially used.
- Curriculum licensing revenue should be excluded from Niagara Assembly revenue share.
- Clarkware pilot or demonstration revenue should be excluded unless the parties later agree on a specific facility-use charge for those activities.

### Payment Rhythm

- Monthly reporting package due within `10 business days` after month end
- Fixed fee and any revenue share due within `15 business days` after month end

## Fit-Out And Improvements

- CLARK leads fit-out of the front two portions tied to training and demonstration use.
- Niagara Assembly leads specialized setup in the reserved back-third hub.
- Moveable CLARK-funded training equipment remains CLARK property unless explicitly transferred.
- Fixed improvements to the building remain with the site unless the definitive agreement states otherwise.

## Liability And Insurance

- CLARK carries instructor liability and training-process compliance risk.
- Niagara Assembly carries facility safety, site procedures, trainee injury, and equipment-damage responsibility at the facility level.
- Each party should maintain commercially reasonable liability insurance relevant to its responsibilities.
- Each party should name the other as additional insured where appropriate and available.

## Drafting Priorities

- attach a marked site plan
- define shared-area access and storage rules
- define reporting format for revenue-share calculation
- define cure periods and insurance minimums
