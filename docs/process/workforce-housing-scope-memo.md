# Workforce Housing Scope Memo

## Decision

Workforce housing is:

- out of scope for the current CLARK venture plan

## Why

- It is not required to prove the training-first launch.
- It is not required to prove Clarkware.
- It is not required to prove partner-facility onboarding economics.
- Keeping it in the live thesis creates adjacency sprawl and weakens investor focus.

## Current Rule

Housing should not appear in the venture overview, business plan, or pitch as part of CLARK's active strategy.

If housing ever re-enters the discussion later, it should be treated as a separate strategic adjacency with its own decision memo, economics, partners, and risk review.
