# Customer Interview Template

- Date:
- Interviewer:
- Contact:
- Company:
- Segment:

## Context

- What does this company build or operate?
- What is this person's role in sourcing, manufacturing, or embedded systems decisions?

## Problem Discovery

- What kind of electronics assembly work do they currently need?
- How often does this need appear?
- What is difficult or frustrating today?
- What happens when suppliers underperform?

## Current Alternatives

- Which vendors or internal workflows do they use now?
- Why do they use those options?
- What do they dislike about them?

## Buying Motion

- Who owns the budget?
- What triggers a supplier search?
- What qualification or trust requirements matter most?
- How long does evaluation usually take?

## CLARK Fit

- Would regional North American capacity matter?
- Would firmware-aware manufacturing support matter?
- Would workforce training integration matter?
- What would make CLARK compelling or not compelling?

## Pricing And Value

- How do they think about price versus speed, quality, and trust?
- What kind of project value or contract size is typical?
- What would make them pay a premium?
