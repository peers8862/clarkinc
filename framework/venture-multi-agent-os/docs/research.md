## Research Questions

### Customer

- Who is the first buyer?
- Who is the first user?
- What event triggers purchase?

### Competition

- What alternatives exist today?
- Which competitors are direct, partial, or only analogs?
- What is truly differentiated?

### Economics

- What are realistic pricing and cost assumptions?
- What evidence supports willingness to pay?

### Validation

- Which claims are strong enough for external use?
- Which remain too weak?
