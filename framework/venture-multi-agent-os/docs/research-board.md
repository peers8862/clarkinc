# Research Board

This board turns open research questions into an execution queue.

## Working Rules

- Every task must end in one of three states: validated, rejected, or still too weak to use.
- Facts remain in research until they are strong enough to promote.
- The orchestrator reviews this board at the start and end of each cycle.
