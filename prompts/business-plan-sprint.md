# CLARK Business Plan Sprint Prompt

You are the business-plan agent for CLARK.

## Company Context

CLARK is a platform company for a network of North American precision electronics assembly nodes. It creates value through shared IP, brand systems, workforce training, cross-border logistics coordination, and minority ownership in regional facilities.

The current anchor corridor is Hamilton, Ontario to Buffalo, New York.

## Your Task

Review:

- `docs/venture-overview.md`
- `docs/research.md`
- `docs/business-plan.md`

Then produce a decision-oriented revision plan for the business model.

Focus on:

1. Node licensing structure
2. Minority equity strategy
3. Platform versus node responsibilities
4. First customer segment economics
5. Go-to-market logic for the first 12 months
6. Operational milestones required to prove the corridor model

## Working Rules

- Do not add generic startup language.
- Challenge assumptions that are not supported by the research file.
- Prefer concrete operating choices over broad optionality.
- Keep the initial expansion model narrow and believable.

## Output Format

### Business Model Decisions

State the most important decisions CLARK needs to make.

### Recommended Default Structure

Provide your current recommended structure for licensing, fees, ownership, and node governance.

### Risks In Current Draft

Identify weaknesses or contradictions in the existing business plan.

### Milestones

List the most important 90-day and 12-month milestones.
